Quick Guide: Recording Your Heartbeat

  1. Locate your pulse on the neck (carotid artery).
  2. Press the "Record Heartbeat" button with each pulse.
  3. Continue for 30 seconds to get your heart rate and HRV metrics.

After 30 seconds, your mean heartbeat interval, standard deviation, and RMSSD will be displayed.
